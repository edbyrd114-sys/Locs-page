Meet the Founder

DeLisha

DeLisha is the founder of Doin' Locs Well, a beauty and personal-care business dedicated to healthy loc styling and natural hair care. Beyond the chair, she's the author of Locs of Love, an early reading advocate, and a philanthropist committed to uplifting her community.

Her mission: help people look good, feel good, and grow — inside and out.
